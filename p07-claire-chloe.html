Explanation bridge

Bank side: start from the bank statement balance, add the deposit-in-transit, then deduct unpresented cheques and the bank deposit error.

Book side: first compute the cash book balance for July: $30,838.50 credit + $98,009.09 deposits - $35,653.49 cheques = $31,517.10 debit. Then add back the over-recorded cheque payment of $81.81 and deduct the unrecorded bank service charge and GIRO rent.

Journal bridge: the business records only items missing or wrong in its own books: service charge, GIRO rent and the Song and Sons cheque error.
