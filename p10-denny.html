Explanation bridge

Land disposal bridge: cash proceeds $3.7m exceed the land carrying amount of $3.3m, so the difference is a $0.4m gain.

Redeemable preference share bridge: redeemable preference shares are treated as a financial obligation. The issue creates cash and a liability; the promised annual return is recorded as finance cost.

Tax bridge: the prior-year $15,000 tax payable is cleared against income tax expense, then the current-year estimate of $200,000 is accrued.

Dividend bridge: ordinary dividend $1.5m × $0.20 = $300,000; outstanding non-redeemable preference dividend $0.6m × ($0.20 - $0.05) = $90,000. Total dividend payable is $390,000.
