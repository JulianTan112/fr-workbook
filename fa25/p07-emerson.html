Explanation for Emerson allowance method

Treatment bridge

Under the allowance method, a specific write-off debits Allowance for Impairment and credits Accounts Receivable. It does not debit impairment expense again because expected loss was recognised through the allowance estimate.

Calculation bridge

The year-end impairment expense is the balancing figure required to make the Allowance T-account end at the required credit balance after considering the opening balance and write-offs. The required allowance balance is not automatically the expense amount.

Ledger self-check

Allowance for Impairment
Opening balance ± write-offs ± adjustment = Required ending credit balance

Missing adjustment = impairment expense or writeback
