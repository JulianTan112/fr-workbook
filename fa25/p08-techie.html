Explanation bridge

The acquisition costs are capitalised because they are required to acquire and install the machine. Depreciation starts from the month the asset is available for use under the nearest-month policy. Since the machine was acquired after the 15th of September, the first year uses October to June, or 9 months.

Double-declining depreciation uses the carrying amount at the beginning of each period, not original cost every year. The residual value is a floor; the asset should not be depreciated below $15,000.
