Explanation bridge

Inventory bridge: the book inventory is $41,400 but the physical count is $40,000, so inventory is credited by $1,400 and the shortage is charged to Cost of goods sold or inventory shortage.

Rent bridge: rent received in advance is $31,200. At $2,400 per month, 12 months have been earned and only October remains unearned, so revenue is $28,800 and unearned rent is $2,400.

Matching principle bridge: supplies used to help generate revenue must be expensed in the same period. If the $940 used is not recorded, supplies on hand would be overstated and supplies expense would be understated.
