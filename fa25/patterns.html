3A. Impairment and contra-account patterns

These terms appear often in the lecture notes. Instead of forcing them into one wide table, read each as a three-part pattern: trigger → SPL effect → SFP presentation.

Receivables

Trigger
Expected uncollectible debts.
SPL effect
Recognise Impairment of Accounts Receivable.
SFP account
Credit Allowance for Impairment of A/R, a contra-asset.
Presentation
Accounts receivable less allowance = net receivables.

Inventory

Trigger
NRV below cost, obsolete inventory, or damaged goods with reduced recoverable value.
SPL effect
Recognise inventory write-down / obsolescence expense, often through COGS.
SFP account
Reduce inventory directly or use Allowance for Inventory Obsolescence.
Presentation
Inventory shown at lower of cost and NRV.

PPE depreciation

Trigger
Use of a non-current asset over its useful life.
SPL effect
Recognise Depreciation Expense.
SFP account
Credit Accumulated Depreciation, a contra-asset.
Presentation
PPE cost less accumulated depreciation = carrying amount.

PPE impairment, if tested

Trigger
Recoverable amount falls below carrying amount.
SPL effect
Recognise Impairment Loss.
SFP account
Use accumulated impairment loss or reduce the asset directly, depending on required treatment.
Presentation
Carrying amount is reduced to recoverable amount.

3B. Profit-measure and tax-language patterns

Use this when a company SPL question asks for tax, interest, operating profit or EBIT-style figures. The key is to place each term in the SPL sequence.

Sequence: Sales − adjusted COGS = Gross profit → add other income → less selling/admin operating expenses = Operating profit / EBIT area → less financial expenses = Profit before tax → less tax expense = Profit after tax.

EBIT

Meaning
Earnings Before Interest and Tax.
Answer treatment
Exclude interest expense and tax expense. Bridge from PBT by adding back interest when needed.

Operating profit

Meaning
Profit from business operations before finance costs and tax.
Answer treatment
Gross profit + other income − selling/admin operating expenses.

Profit before tax

Meaning
Profit after financial expenses but before tax.
Answer treatment
Use as base before deducting income tax expense.

Taxable income

Meaning
Amount subject to tax under tax rules.
Answer treatment
Tax = taxable income × tax rate, unless the question gives a different tax amount.

Tax expense

Meaning
SPL expense line.
Answer treatment
Current-year tax ± prior-year adjustment.

Tax payable

Meaning
SFP current liability.
Answer treatment
Credit Tax Payable when tax is recognised; debit when paid or overestimated.
