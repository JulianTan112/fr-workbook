Explanation bridge

Under the cost model, fair-value changes are ignored and the building remains at cost less accumulated depreciation until disposal. Under the revaluation model, the 31 December 20X8 carrying amount is reset to fair value after depreciation is recorded and accumulated depreciation is eliminated. On disposal, the revaluation reserve related to the asset is transferred directly to retained earnings.
