Sunshine Pte Ltd — GST journals and control accounts

GST control account answer bridge

AccountOfficial answer bridgeAmount
GST receivableInput GST claims after purchase returns and claimable items52,218 Dr then transferred
GST payableOutput GST after sales returns and local asset disposal97,128 Cr then transferred
Net GSTPayable to tax authority44,910 Cr
Core self-check: net GST payable = output GST $97,128 - input GST $52,218 = $44,910.

Explanation bridge

Output GST bridge: local taxable sales and local taxable disposal of office equipment create GST payable. Export sales are zero-rated and do not create output GST.

Input GST bridge: local taxable purchases, claimable insurance, office rental, machinery and the claimable local telephone component create GST receivable. Purchase returns reverse input GST.

Net GST bridge: transfer both control accounts to Net GST. If output GST is larger than input GST, the balance is payable.
