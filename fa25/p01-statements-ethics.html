Explanation for statement relationships and ethics cases

Accounting bridge

SPL profit → retained earnings movement in equity
Paying a payable → Dr Accounts Payable / Cr Cash
Impairment → expense in SPL + reduced asset carrying amount in SFP

The statements link together: profit from the SPL is not isolated; it flows into retained earnings. Paying a payable reduces both liability and cash. Impairment reduces profit and prevents asset overstatement.

Ethics bridge

Pressure to defer a loss creates an objectivity/intimidation threat. Reviewing one’s own valuation creates a self-review threat. The debit/credit may still be mechanically correct, but the safeguard is independent review and faithful representation.
