Statement elements and assumption classifier

Classification schedule

Delivery truck / prepaid insurance

ElementAssets

Controlled resources with expected future benefit.

Bank loan / unpaid utilities

ElementLiabilities

Present obligations from past events.

Share capital

ElementEquity

Owner claim after deducting liabilities.

Service revenue / wage expense

ElementIncome / Expense

Performance and cost effects reported in SPL.

Owner’s personal home

AssumptionBusiness entity assumption

Exclude unless owned/controlled by the business.

Explanation for statement-element classification

Classification bridge

Delivery truck / prepaid insurance → Assets: future economic benefit remains
Bank loan / unpaid utilities        → Liabilities: present obligations exist
Share capital                       → Equity: owner claim
Service revenue / wage expense      → SPL items: income and expense
Owner personal home                 → Excluded: not business entity asset

Classify by definition first, then assign the statement. Assets, liabilities and equity sit in the SFP. Income and expenses go to the SPL. Owner/private items are excluded because the business entity is separate from the owner.
