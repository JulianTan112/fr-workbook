Question / case data

DateTransaction
Apr 1Chris started the business with $50,000 cash and a computer costing $3,000.
Apr 2Purchased a second-hand lorry for $80,000, paying $20,000 down-payment and obtaining a bank loan for the remainder.
Apr 6Purchased supplies for $1,200 on credit from Tiong Seng Supplies.
Apr 8Agreed to provide $30,000 worth of moving services for Post Trading in June 20X2.
Apr 10Billed customers for packing and moving services $16,000.
Apr 15Paid for supplies purchased on 6 April.
Apr 27Paid wages to workers $3,600 and $2,000 to Chris for personal expenses.
Apr 30Received a bill for petrol purchased and used in April, $800, payable in May.

Required

  • Prepare General Journal entries.
  • Post to General Ledger/T-accounts.
  • Prepare Trial Balance.
  • Prepare Statement of Profit or Loss and Statement of Financial Position.
