Explanation bridge

Bank side:
$17,350 + $900 − $2,000 = $16,250

Books side:
$11,100 + $5,250 + $800 − $700 − $200 = $16,250

Interest component of AC Enterprise EFT:
$5,000 × 10% × 6 / 12 = $250

Why Cheque #4 is not adjusted: it was written in June and has now cleared the bank. It has already done its job in the bank statement and does not require a new October book entry.

Why Cheque #8 is deducted from bank side: the business has already recorded the payment, but the bank has not processed it.
