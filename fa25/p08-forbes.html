Explanation bridge

Trade discount reduces the asset cost. Delivery and installation are directly attributable. The employee-caused damage repair is not necessary to bring the asset to intended use, so it is expensed rather than capitalised. The damaged unit is depreciated up to 30 September before disposal; since proceeds are below carrying amount, the difference is a loss.
