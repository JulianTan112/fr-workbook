Explanation bridge

Rental bridge: $24,000 covers six months from August to January. By 31 December, five months are earned, so revenue is $24,000 / 6 × 5 = $20,000 and the remaining liability is $4,000.

Interest bridge: annual interest is $400,000 × 10% = $40,000. Because interest is payable half-yearly and the July to December portion is unpaid at year-end, accrue $40,000 × 6 / 12 = $20,000.

Statement bridge: only liability balances are required in part (b): the bank loan remains non-current, while accounts payable, unearned rental revenue, interest payable and accrued repairs are current liabilities.
