Adjustment facts
No.Source factAdjustment trigger
1Salaries are paid every Monday. 31 December 2023 falls on a Sunday. Staff earn $1,500 for a five-day work week.Accrued salaries expense.
2Green Company paid $12,000 on 31 August for six months of consulting services beginning 1 October at $2,000 per month.Unearned revenue earned for October to December.
3Business insurance was prepaid for six months from 1 November. Prepaid Insurance balance is $7,800.Expired insurance for November and December.
4Advertising bill of $1,200 received on 31 December and payable on 10 January.Accrued advertising payable.
5Services worth $28,000 completed for Blue Company by 31 December; invoice sent after year-end.Accrued service revenue and receivable.
6Computers: $75,000 acquired on 1 January, plus $30,000 acquired on 1 July. Useful life is five years, no residual value.Depreciation for full-year and half-year assets.
7Office supplies remaining at 31 December are $2,400.Supplies used must be expensed.
8Grass cutting for $500 completed on 31 December and unpaid.Accrued miscellaneous expense and payable.

Required

  1. Journalise adjusting entries (1) to (8) on 31 December 2023.
  2. Prepare the adjusted trial balance.
  3. Prepare the adjusted Income Statement, Statement of Changes in Equity and Balance Sheet.
