Question or case data

Bali Hut’s Cash at Bank ledger had a debit balance of $16,940.60 at 31 March 2006. During April, total deposits were $121,830.20 and cheques issued were $89,105.50. The Maybank statement showed a credit balance of $53,775.25 at 30 April 2006.

Difference found at month-endAmount ($)
Cashier deposited receipts on 30 Apr; not shown in April bank statement.3,100.55
Cheque payment to Sukarno Trading was recorded in Cash Payments Journal as $1,940 instead of $1,490.450.00 error
Outstanding cheque no. 2325 dated 25 Apr.5,200.10
Outstanding cheque no. 2333 dated 29 Apr.846.25
EFT receipt from Ginger Garden credited by bank but not recorded in cash books.3,600.90
Cheque received from DNT Enterprise returned insufficient funds.2,850.75
Bank service charge shown only on bank statement.36.00
Case-data check

Bank reconciliation source facts retained: bank-side items, book-side items, bank errors, EFT, dishonoured cheque and bank charges.

Required

  • Prepare the Bank Reconciliation Statement at 30 April 2006.
  • Prepare the necessary book-side journal entries.
  • Show which items require no journal entry.
