Additional information

Net income is $107,000 and depreciation expense is $54,000. Old equipment was sold at book value: both cost and accumulated depreciation were $47,900, so no cash was received and no gain or loss was recorded. New equipment and long-term investments were paid in cash. Dividends were declared and fully paid.
