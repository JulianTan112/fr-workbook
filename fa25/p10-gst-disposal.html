Question / case data

ItemAmount / rate
Cost of machinery$80,000
Accumulated depreciation$10,000
Sales proceeds excluding GST$75,000
GST rate9%

The company is GST-registered and disposes of the machine for cash/credit to a local buyer.

Required

  1. Calculate the carrying amount of the machine.
  2. Calculate the gain or loss on disposal before GST.
  3. Calculate the GST payable/output tax.
  4. Prepare the General Journal entry.
  5. Show ledger closure for Machinery and Accumulated Depreciation.

4. Explanation for the common confusion

The 9% is not multiplied twice. The Cash/Receivables line shows the invoice total: $75,000 × 109% = $81,750. The GST Payable line shows the GST part inside that invoice: $75,000 × 9% = $6,750. The gain uses the GST-exclusive selling price: $75,000 - $70,000 = $5,000.
