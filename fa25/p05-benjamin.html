Explanation bridge

Commission bridge: the unearned revenue account starts at $18,000. If $8,000 remains unearned, $10,000 has been earned and is transferred to commission revenue.

Rental bridge: prepaid rent starts at $450,000 and $90,000 is still unexpired, so $360,000 is shop rental expense.

Interest bridge: annual interest is $300,000 × 5% = $15,000. Since $11,250 is already in interest expense, the missing accrual is $3,750.

Statement bridge: shop-related expenses are selling expenses; office salaries and telephone are administrative expenses; interest is a financial expense.
