Explanation for Rivera Roofing statement preparation

Profit bridge

Service revenue:
$7,600 cash service + $8,200 service on account + $5,000 billed = $20,800

Expenses:
Salary $1,560 + utilities $295 = $1,855

Net profit:
$20,800 − $1,855 = $18,945

Cash and receivables bridge

Cash:
$80,000 − $1,000 − $600 + $7,600 − $2,300 + $8,200 − $1,560 − $295 − $1,800 = $88,245

Accounts receivable:
$8,200 + $5,000 − $8,200 = $5,000

SFP bridge

Assets:
Cash $88,245 + Accounts Receivable $5,000 + Supplies $3,700 + Office equipment $2,300 + Roofing equipment $5,000 = $104,245

Equity + liabilities:
Capital $80,000 + profit $18,945 − drawings $1,800 + Accounts Payable $7,100 = $104,245

Self-check: drawings reduce equity, not expenses; credit purchases create Accounts Payable until paid.
