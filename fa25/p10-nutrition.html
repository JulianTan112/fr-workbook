Explanation for Nutrition Foods adjustments

Adjustment bridge

Shophouse recognition:
Use asset fair value = $750,000, not share market total $780,000.

Shophouse depreciation:
$750,000 / 20 × 2 / 12 = $6,250

Directors’ fees accrual:
$10,000 × 3 = $30,000

Tax / dividend / allowance bridge

Tax:
Dr Tax Payable $28,800 / Cr Income Tax Expense $28,800 to clear prior year.
Dr Income Tax Expense $32,400 / Cr Tax Payable $32,400 for current year.

Dividend payable:
Ordinary: (600,000 + 300,000) × $0.10 = $90,000
Preference unpaid: 200,000 × ($0.30 − $0.10) = $40,000
Total dividend payable = $130,000

Allowance:
Required allowance = 10% × $430,000 = $43,000
Adjustment = $43,000 − $40,200 = $2,800

Statement bridge

Profit after tax = $604,350
Retained earnings = $614,300 + $604,350 − $130,000 = $1,088,650
SFP check: total assets $4,231,050 = total equity and liabilities $4,231,050
