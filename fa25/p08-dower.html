Accounting workings

Depreciation expense for 20X4

Remaining useful life at 31 Dec 20X3 = 6 years − 9 months = 5 years 3 months = 63 months
Monthly depreciation after revaluation = 220,000 / 63 = 3,492.063
20X4 depreciation = 3,492.063 × 12 = 41,904.76 ≈ 41,905

Explanation bridge

Revaluation is performed after recording depreciation up to the revaluation date. The asset is then carried at fair value. Future depreciation is based on the revalued amount over the remaining useful life.
